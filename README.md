
# Superstore Sales Performance Analysis (Power BI Dashboard)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales performance of a retail superstore using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal is to convert raw sales data into meaningful insights that help understand business performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments.

The dashboard allows users to explore trends in sales, profit, and quantity sold, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions for improving sales strategies and operational efficiency.

---

## Problem Statement

Retail businesses generate large volumes of sales data, but extracting valuable insights from this data can be challenging without proper visualization and analysis tools. Businesses need an effective way to track performance, identify profitable segments, and detect areas that require improvement.

This project aims to analyze Superstore sales data and build an interactive dashboard that helps visualize key metrics and uncover important business insights.

---

## Proposed Solution

An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to analyze sales and profit across multiple dimensions including region, state, product category, customer segment, and time period.

The dashboard provides filtering and drill-down capabilities, allowing users to explore data dynamically and gain deeper insights into business performance.

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance.

Key metrics displayed:

* **Total Sales:** $2.30M
* **Total Profit:** 286K
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 38K

Visualizations include:

* Sales and profit trends by quarter
* Sales distribution by year and quarter
* Sales contribution by customer segment
* Profit contribution by product category

---

### 2. Region and State Level Analysis

Analyzes geographical sales performance.

Features include:

* Region and year filters
* Sales breakdown by state
* City-level quantity and profit analysis
* Category-wise sales comparison across states

Insights include:

* Top performing states
* Cities generating the highest profits
* Regional sales distribution

---

### 3. Category and Sub-Category Analysis

Examines product-level performance.

Features include:

* Category and sub-category filters
* Sales vs quantity relationship
* Profit trends by quarter
* Segment-wise quantity distribution

Insights include:

* Best performing product categories
* High demand sub-categories
* Profit trends across years and quarters

---

## Key Insights

Important insights derived from the dashboard include:

* The **Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales (~50%)**
* The **Technology category generates the highest profit**
* **California and New York are the top performing states**
* Some cities generate **negative profit despite strong sales**, indicating potential pricing or discount issues
